What to expect?

The consultation is a great chance to meet Carolina and ask any of the questions you may have about the process.

If you can’t think of any questions, there is nothing to worry about as all the science will be explained to you in an easy to understand way. Carolina really knows laser hair removal inside and out. By the time you come out of the consultation, you’ll be an expert.Carolina will cover all of the below.

-How the laser works

-Who can and can’t have the treatment

-How long the treatment will last for

-How many treatments you will need

-How long the treatment will take

-Potential side effects of the laser

The Patch Test

The patch test is an essential part of keeping you safe throughout the process. Although it is rare, there are some people whose skin does not respond well to the laser treatment. During your consultation, Carolina will administer the laser on a small section of the planned treatment area. Over the next 24 to 48 hours, you would see any side affects present themselves on the skin. Once the patch test is done, and there are no side effects, you’re good to go.

Pre and Post treatment planning

Once you’ve had your patch test, and you're good to go, there are some preparation takes to take care of before coming in for your treatment. In the consultation, Carolina will make sure you know everything you need to do before coming in.

You will also be given a post-treatment plan. This will give you all the advice you need to look after your skin after your treatment. Carolina will also go through this with you to make sure you're fully equipped to keep your skin healthy and safe.
