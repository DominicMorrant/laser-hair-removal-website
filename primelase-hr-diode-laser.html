Why does the laser matter?

Finally, we have a truly permanent hair removal laser, thanks to the high absorption wavelength and shot pulse duration that heats and destroys the hair follicle. This is all possible due to laser targeting of the melanin surrounding the hair follicle. This targeted laser will destroy the melanin and kill the hair follicle. This is a revolution since until this technology was offered, laser hair removal has only damaged the melanin, meaning the hair would grow back in time. So, although laser hair removal may seem like a large investment, it will become a saving as you can say goodbye to waxing and shaving for good. Theres also the benefit of not having to do the shaving or suffer the waxing. Carolina guarantees that treated hair follicles will not return for 10 years. Please do discuss the permanence with her on consultation as there are a few ways that hairs can return which need to be discussed.

6 sessions are all it takes to complete the treatment because of the power of the technology. Until now many laser hair removal treatments have recommended double this number of treatments. This would mean more cost to you and more inconvenience. Even when you had completed the treatment, this would only be optimal results and not permanent results. The Primelase HR Diode Laser is quicker and more affective.

The Primelase HR Diode Laser also uses a cooling feature. The benefit of this is higher laser power without the risk of burning. With that higher power comes better, faster results. This also means that the laser is safer than the other offerings on the market.
